Quadrant Knowledge Solutions’ SPARK Matrix is a tool that evaluates vendors based on technology excellence and customer impact. The SPARK Matrix report also consists of the market overview, key capabilities, differentiators, and vendor profiles.

Strategically evaluating vendor’s performance and assisting in competitive positioning and ranking, the full form of SPARK stands for Strategic Performance Assessment & Ranking.

Quadrant Knowledge Solutions’ SPARK matrix tool assists vendors and end users in competitive positioning which helps them to stand out amongst their competitors and provides brand visibility for their business.

Quadrant Knowledge Solutions’ SPARK matrix is a global tool encompassing regions like USA, Canada, Latin America, Western Europe, Central & Eastern Europe, Middle East & Africa, AxJC, Japan and China.

Quadrant Knowledge Solutions’ SPARK matrix tool covers 21 domains in total including - Application Development, BPM & Process Automation, CX and MarTech, Cloud Management, Communication & Collaboration, Data, Analytics, and AI, Data Management Service, Enterprise IT Services, Enterprise Architecture Planning, Financial Crime & Compliance, HRTech, Information Security, IT Infrastructure Networking, Integrated Risk Management, IoT & Digitalization, Procurement Management, Project & Portfolio Management, Privacy Data Management, Retail & eCommerce and lastly Supply Chain Management.

Quadrant Knowledge Solutions’ SPARK Matrix Tool can be beneficial to ICT vendors and end users who are looking for strategic positioning of their business based on their technology excellence and customer impact in the marketplace.
