The Vendor WHAO Assessment is an objective evaluation conducted by QKS, an unbiased analyst firm. It provides a third-party vendor-neutral assessment of a vendor's specific IT product deployment, evaluating key parameters to identify strengths and improvement opportunities.

The Vendor WHAO Assessment offers several benefits for vendors. It enhances their credibility through an independent validation of their strengths. It provides a competitive advantage, helps identify areas for improvement, and serves as a valuable marketing and sales tool.

End-users can leverage the Vendor WHAO Assessment to gain an unbiased and objective assessment of vendors. It helps them evaluate if their investments are safe, understand a vendor's product roadmap, and make informed decisions when selecting IT solutions.

The Vendor WHAO Assessment evaluates vendors based on parameters such as technical competence (specific competencies and addons/differentiators), strategy, organizational viability, and customer impact.

QKS is an unbiased analyst firm known for its neutrality and expertise. They follow a rigorous evaluation process, using established methodologies to ensure objectivity and credibility in their assessments.

Yes, vendors can leverage the Vendor WHAO Assessment as a powerful marketing tool. The assessment report provides validation from a trusted third party, enhancing brand image, customer loyalty, and overall sales.
