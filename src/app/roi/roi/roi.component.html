The ROI benchmark report is a vendor-neutral demonstration of value derived from product deployment which empowers vendors to validate their investments and end users to truly understand the benefits, costs, and return on investment associated with adopting a specific vendor's offering.

The ROI benchmark report is used to calculate the ROI and payback period ensuring the investment of vendors and end-users are validated. By validating the investment, Quadrant Knowledge solution offers unique value proposition and compelling advantage to the solution or product in question.

The ROI benchmark report provides validation from Quadrant Knowledge Solutions, serving as a certificate of authenticity for vendors ROI claims that become a powerful marketing tool. We also ensure transparency with TCO analysis so that there will be a clear understanding of the financial implications of the solutions.

The process involves four essential steps following project initiation:

• Questionnaire is designed to conduct in-depth interviews with 3-4 customers within the target segment.
• The second step is to create target profile after the customer interviews, which serves as the foundation for all future calculations.
• Benefit and cost profile is created to further refine analysis.
• The ROI and payback period is calculated is calculated ensuring the approach delivers optimal results.

In the planned roadmap section, we proactively address the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead for both the vendor and the customer. We analyze and anticipate future trends and developments to help chart a course for long-term success. This helps tie everything together and provide expert recommendations based on insights gained from the report.
